In this experiment, you will be asked to respond to a target while viewing images. There will be 2 parts with separate instructions.

In the second part, you could earn real money. Responding quickly to the target earns 5¢. The faster you respond, the more likely you are to win. You could win between $1 and $3 for good performance.

We reserve the right to withhold payment of the bonus for poor performance. Please do not respond continuously. Please try not to miss any responses. If you miss too many responses, or have impossibly fast responses, or have otherwise very poor performance, we will withhold payment of the bonus.

It is important that you pay close attention in order to earn the maximum possible bonus. That is, the closer you pay attention, the more money you could win.
